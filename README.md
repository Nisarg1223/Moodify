# 🎵 Moodify - Mood-Based Music Recommendation Website

**Moodify** is a modern web application that detects a user's facial expression through the webcam, identifies their current mood, and recommends songs that match that emotion. The goal is to make music discovery more personal by connecting emotions with the perfect playlist.

Whether you're feeling happy, sad, angry, surprised, or relaxed, Moodify instantly suggests songs that suit your mood, creating a seamless and enjoyable listening experience.

## ✨ Features

* 😊 Real-Time Face Detection
* 🎭 Mood Detection using Facial Expressions
* 🎶 Song Recommendations Based on Detected Mood
* 📚 Built-in Tutorial for New Users
* ℹ️ About Section
* 🕒 Detection & Recommendation History
* ⬆️ Upload Your Own Songs
* 📱 Fully Responsive Design
* ⚡ Modern and User-Friendly Interface

## 🚀 How It Works

1. Open Moodify in your browser.
2. Allow camera access.
3. The application detects your face and identifies your facial expression.
4. Based on the detected mood, relevant songs are recommended.
5. Enjoy music that matches your current emotion.

## 💡 Motivation

Music often reflects our emotions. Moodify aims to make music selection effortless by automatically recommending songs that align with the user's current mood, providing a more engaging and personalized listening experience.
